About the Artist

Joseph David Bennett is a multi-instrumentalist, songwriter, and producer whose music blends introspective lyrics with genre-blurring soundscapes. Rooted in raw emotion and sonic experimentation, his work explores identity, transformation, and the search for meaning in a fragmented world. Drawing from electronic, alternative, and folk influences, Bennett crafts immersive tracks that feel as personal as they are universal. Whether alone with a guitar or surrounded by layered production, his sound invites listeners into a space that's both intimate and expansive.

About the Song

Tomorrow is a lush, introspective journey through the fog of identity, uncertainty, and transformation. Written and performed by Joseph David Bennett, the track blends ambient textures with raw lyrical vulnerability, asking weighty questions like "what if there is no me to be searching for?" Produced in collaboration with 9 Theory and Jonathan Killian, Tomorrow moves between acoustic intimacy and electronic transcendence. It's a sonic reflection on breaking through emotional inertia, staying stuck in cycles of delay and self-doubt, and the quiet hope that freedom might still lie just beyond the next dawn.
